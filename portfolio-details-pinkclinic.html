Pink Clinic Project Details

Developing a discreet, professional, and user-friendly digital platform for Nigeria's leading private sexual health clinic.

Pink Clinic – A Safe Haven for Sexual Health

Pink Clinic is dedicated to breaking the stigma surrounding sexual health in Nigeria. The goal was to build a website that feels like a 'safe space'—private, non-judgmental, and secure. We focused on a clean, modern aesthetic that appeals to a younger, tech-savvy demographic while maintaining the clinical professionalism required for a healthcare provider.

The platform serves as a comprehensive hub for sexual wellness. It features detailed sections for STI screening packages, birth control options, and vitamin therapy. We implemented a streamlined navigation system that allows users to find sensitive information quickly and book appointments discreetly, ensuring their privacy is protected from the first click.

By integrating empathetic copywriting with a 'privacy-first' design, we helped Pink Clinic establish a strong online presence. The site successfully handles inquiries for both their Mainland and Island Lagos locations, providing a seamless bridge between virtual consultation and physical care.
