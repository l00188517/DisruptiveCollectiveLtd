Pricing Detailed Breakdown.

We believe in "no surprises."

See exactly how we work and how our pricing are structured.

01

Strategy & Discovery

Cost: $1,000 – $2,000+

Before a single pixel is moved, an agency performs research to ensure the site will actually convert visitors.

  • Competitor Analysis: Researching what your rivals are doing right/wrong.
  • User Personas: Defining exactly who the audience is.
  • Sitemap & Information Architecture: Planning the hierarchy of pages.
  • Stakeholder Interviews: Aligning the site with your business goals.

02

UI/UX Design (The Visuals)

Cost: $1,000 – $1,500+

This is the "blueprinting" phase. Agencies typically charge based on the number of unique page templates (e.g., the Home page is one template, a Blog post is another).

  • Wireframing: Low-fidelity sketches of layout and functionality.
  • UI Design: High-fidelity mockups including colors, typography, and branding.
  • Interactive Prototypes: A clickable version of the design to test user flow before coding begins.

03

Development (The Build)

Cost: $1,500 – $4,500+

This is usually the most expensive phase. It involves turning the design into a functional, responsive website.

  • CMS Integration: Setting up platforms like WordPress, Webflow, or Shopify so you can edit content easily.
  • Front-End Development: Coding the visual parts (HTML/CSS/JS) and ensuring mobile responsiveness.
  • Back-End Development: security, and custom functionalities (calculators).
  • DB Development: Setting up databases (member portals).

04

Content & SEO

Cost: $1,000 – $1,500+

A beautiful site is useless if it’s empty or invisible to Google.

  • Copywriting: Professional SEO-optimized writing for your core pages.
  • Asset Sourcing: Buying stock photography or editing your provided images/videos.
  • On-Page SEO: Metadata, alt-text, URL structuring, and keyword mapping.

05

Testing, QA & Launch

Cost: $500 – $1,500

Agencies perform rigorous testing to ensure the site doesn't break on launch day.

  • Browser Testing: Ensuring it works on Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge.
  • Device Testing: Testing on various iPhones, Androids, and tablets.
  • Speed Optimization: Compressing images and clean coding for fast load times.
  • Deployment: Moving the site from a "staging" area to your live domain.

06

Accessibility & Compliance

Cost: $1,000 – $1,500+ (or +15-20% of the total project cost)

The price varies depending on whether we are "building for compliance" from scratch or "remediating" an existing site.

  • WCAG 2.1/2.2 Audit: A manual and automated review of the site to ensure it meets "AA" standards (the global benchmark for legal compliance).
  • Keyboard Navigation: Ensuring the entire site can be used without a mouse, including "Skip to Content" links and visible focus indicators (the boxes that appear around buttons when you tab).
  • Screen Reader Optimization: Adding ARIA labels and semantic HTML so that software for the visually impaired can correctly describe what is on the screen (e.g., "Submit Button" instead of just "Button").
  • Color Contrast & Visuals: Testing all text against background colors to ensure a minimum contrast ratio (typically 4.5:1) for users with low vision or color blindness.
  • Alt-Text & Media: Writing descriptive text for every image and ensuring all videos have Closed Captions (CC) and, in some cases, audio descriptions.
  • Legal Documentation: Many agencies provide an Accessibility Statement or a VPAT (Voluntary Product Accessibility Template) for your footer to prove you are actively maintaining standards.

Frequently Asked Questions

Transparent pricing designed to scale with your ambition.

Can I customize a plan?

Absolutely. While the 'Ignite' plan has a fixed scope, our 'Accelerate' and 'Enterprise' plans are fully customizable to meet your specific needs. We recommend the Enterprise plan for bespoke solutions.

How does the monthly retainer work?

The 'Accelerate' retainer gives you access to our team for a set number of hours per month. This provides flexibility to work on various tasks, from new features and bug fixes to strategic planning, all for a predictable monthly cost.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. We believe in full transparency. All project costs are detailed in a comprehensive proposal before any work begins. Any third-party costs, such as cloud hosting or software licenses, will be clearly outlined and approved by you.

What's the difference between Project-Based and a Retainer?

A project-based plan ('Ignite') is for a single, well-defined deliverable with a fixed price. A retainer ('Accelerate') is a monthly subscription that gives you ongoing access to our team for a variety of evolving tasks, providing flexibility and continuous support.

Which services are included in the 'Ignite' plan?

The Ignite plan is built around a core Web or Mobile Development project. It includes all the necessary steps to take that single project from concept to launch, including UI/UX design, development, and project management.
